# Advanced Linear Algebra Project

## Learning Objectives
- Compute determinants of matrices.
- Understand **minors**, **cofactors**, and **adjugates** of matrices and calculate them.
- Understand the concept of a matrix **inverse** and compute it.
- Understand **eigenvalues** and **eigenvectors** and calculate them.
- Understand the **definiteness** of a matrix and determine whether a matrix is positive definite, negative definite, or indefinite.
